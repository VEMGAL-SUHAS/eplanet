6-Stage Quality Control Process

From refinery to pump — every step monitored and certified

Source Verification

Fuel sourced directly from authorized refineries with complete traceability. Every tanker carries a unique QR code for digital tracking from refinery to pump.

Laboratory Testing

NABL accredited labs test every batch for density, flash point, sulphur content, octane/cetane numbers, and water contamination before dispatch.

Tanker Sealing

Tamper-evident electronic seals with GPS tracking. Any breach triggers immediate alerts. Temperature monitoring ensures fuel integrity during transit.

Storage Monitoring

Underground tanks equipped with automatic tank gauging, leak detection sensors, and water sump alarms. Daily dip measurements ensure accurate inventory.

Dispenser Calibration

Fuel dispensers calibrated every 6 months by Legal Metrology Department. Flow meters tested for accuracy within ±0.3% tolerance as per OIML standards.

Customer Assurance

Free density testing available at every pump. Customers can verify fuel quality on-site. Complaint resolution within 24 hours guaranteed.
